3. Normalization (Log Transform)

Used when data is skewed (not symmetric).

Log transform reduces large values and makes distribution more normal.

Example: np.log(column).

Why Important

Prevents one feature from dominating others

Speeds up model training

Improves accuracy and convergence

Required for many ML algorithms
